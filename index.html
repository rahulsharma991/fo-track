"Fraud occurs in all sectors in this world now. One of the fraud such as Food Fraud that includes the act of purposely altering, misreading, misleading, substituting or tampering with any food product at any point along the farm-to table food supply-chain. A blockchain system is introduced which lets consumer to know the origin of its food he is consuming as well as certain details that includes it manufacturer, origin of food, number of suppliers issued it as well as dates and time.

Farm chain traces food products from origin to final consumer using blockchain technology.​ Blockchain is a public, distributed and decentralized ledger, where information is encrypted and stored in a tamper-proof and secure way. Once data is entered into the system and validated, it becomes unalterable. IoT (Internet of Things) enabled devices is used to implement traceability that covers each phase of the supply chain including production, logistics and distribution.​
